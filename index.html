Hey there! A computer helps us everyday of our lives. Seems like a mega-mind, does it not? Four, brave people dared to venture into the dusty depths. Kavithan, Lucy, Jimmy, and Anish. We completely took apart the computer. Our tasks was to adopt a lonely computer and explore the various hardware it had within its system. We then identified and labeled the parts and recorded our findings. Afterwards, me managed to find all of the parts again, and put them in the right spots. We learnt how they interact, to make a computer work the way it does. We have provided a description, so you too, may understand what goes on in AI's brain.
